About The Event

About The Event

ROBOHACKS is a hackathon that is open to all and intended to give participants experience with robotics. As the field of robotics is growing, we want to connect individuals interested in robotics and programing in a fun and competitive way.

For two weeks you will get the chance to immerse yourself in robotic by participating in our workshops, and competing in our challenges. Take the chance to bring your ideas to life!

When

January 17 - January 28
You will get the chance to form teams, participate in workshops, conceptualize and order parts

January 28 - January 30
Use your skills and work with your team to assemble a robot for judging day

Challenges

January 28 @ 5:00pm - January 30 @ 12:00pm

We get it! Building a robot requires hardware and one's robot can be quite different from others. Instead of loaning you electronics that you won't need we will finance your robot up to $100 per team. You will purchase your own parts with your team and on judging day, after you have competed in your desired challenges, you will disassemble your robot, submit your parts to our team, and we will refund you!

F.A.Qs

  • First time participating in a hackathon?

    RoboHack welcomes students of all skill levels. We will have workshops and mentors to help you with your project. Hackathons can be a great place to learn new skills in a short period of time; first hand experience, in building robotic hardware and programming it to perform real-world challenges. Just be eager to learn, and excited to meet lots of awesome people.

  • Is the hackathon online or in-person?

    The workshops, team formation, and conceptualization will be virtual during January 17th to January 28th. From January 28th to January 30th, the hackathon will be in-person and hackers need to meet up at University of Toronto Scarborough and follow the public health guidelines around COVID-19. Stay tuned to hear the latest updates by following our social media channels.

  • Can I participate as a volunteer in this hackathon?

    Yes! But note that if you get accepted as a hacker, you can’t be both a hacker and a volunteer. If you are interested, please apply here.

  • What are the prizes and cost involved for the challenges?

    There are $5,000+ in prizes! Admission to RoboHack is completely free! We will provide all the resources and mentorship our hackers need to build something incredible. Weekend worth of meals, drinks and snacks will be available during the hackathon. You will purchase your own parts with your team and after competing in your chosen challenge(s), you can either keep your robot or disassemble the robot and submit your parts to our team to be refunded up to $100 for your team. More details can be found in part 4 of the application form.

  • Max people allowed to form a team?

    You can form teams of 3 or 4 members. There are no restrictions for who they can be, so you can team up with hackers from any school, country, or experience level. Teams can be formed before or during the week of January 17th. However, all members of the team must be accepted as candidates after filling out the application form.

  • How to become a Sponsor?

    If you are interested in sponsoring our event, please email us at contact@robohacks.ca and we will get back to you about the available sponsorship opportunities and discuss with you what you have in mind.
